1. Executive Summary

ETMT (Electro Mata) is a community-driven BEP-20 token built on the BNB Smart Chain (BSC). The project focuses on transparency, security, sustainable growth, and long-term ecosystem development.


With a fixed supply, 0% Buy Tax, and 0% Sell Tax, ETMT aims to provide a simple, efficient, and accessible digital asset for holders and investors.

2. Vision

To establish ETMT as a trusted and globally recognized blockchain ecosystem driven by innovation, transparency, and community growth.

3. Mission

  • Build a strong global community
  • Expand ecosystem adoption
  • Strengthen liquidity and market accessibility
  • Support long-term value creation
  • Maintain transparency and security

6. Liquidity Strategy

60% of the total supply is allocated to liquidity development.

Liquidity will be deployed gradually in multiple phases to:

  • Improve market stability
  • Increase trading depth
  • Reduce volatility
  • Support sustainable growth
  • Strengthen investor confidence

A portion of liquidity may be locked through recognized liquidity-locking platforms to enhance transparency and trust.
